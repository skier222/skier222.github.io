ABOUT US

The Skier Massage is the idea of a group of athletes and engineers. As life-long downhill skiers, we all discovered late the benefits of trigger point therapy and post-workout massages. Only a few years ago did we regularly employ the exact same do-it-yourself rolling massage treatment that the Skier Massage provides to mitigate our myofascial soreness after all physical activities, including long days on the mountain. On a trip to Summit County, Colorado with college friends, one of us began to grasp the potential utility of such a rolling massage device for downhill skiers. The idea of producing a personal masseuse for skiers was born.


Don't be shy! Check us out on Instagram. Otherwise, we'll see you out on the mountain!
